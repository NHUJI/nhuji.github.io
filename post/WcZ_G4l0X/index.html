English:

Privacy Policy

This privacy policy is designed to help you understand how we handle any personal information that is associated with you.

Third-Party Service Providers
Our app does not include any third-party tracking or data collection services.

Data Protection
We have taken appropriate security measures to protect your personal information from loss, misuse, unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.

Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We recommend you review this page periodically for any changes.

Contact Us
If you have any questions about our privacy policy, please contact us by email at hujinfinite@gmail.com.
